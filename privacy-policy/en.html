Privacy Policy for Shevet App

Effective Date: November 1st, 2023

Welcome to Shevet, a social networking app that allows users to post their status updates and view the statuses of other users they have approved. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our app.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand how we handle your information. By using Shevet, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. User-Provided Information

When you register for an account on Shevet, we collect the following information from you:

  • Your phone number.
  • Profile picture (optional).
  • Status updates and content you post.

1.2. Information You Share

Shevet is a social networking app, and you have the option to share status updates and other content with users you have approved as your friends or connections.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1. Providing and Improving the Service

We use the information you provide to create and manage your account and to provide you with access to Shevet. We also use your data to enhance the app's functionality, features, and user experience.

2.2. Communication

We may use your phone number to send you important updates and notifications about your account and the app's features. You can control the type of notifications you receive in your account settings.

2.3. Status Updates

Your status updates are shared with users you have approved. We do not use your status updates or your phone number for any marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1. Data Storage

We store your status updates and phone number on our secure servers. Your data is stored in accordance with industry-standard security protocols to protect it from unauthorized access and breaches.

3.2. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you have an active account on Shevet. You can delete your account at any time, which will also delete your associated data.

4. Sharing Your Information

4.1. Status Updates

Your status updates are only visible to users you have approved. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other purposes.

5. Your Choices

5.1. Account Information

You can update and edit your account information at any time through the app's settings.

5.2. Notification Preferences

You can control the types of notifications you receive through the app's settings.

5.3. Data Deletion

You can delete your Shevet account at any time, which will also delete your associated data.

6. Children's Privacy

Shevet is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that we have unintentionally collected information from a child, please contact us to have the data removed.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the privacy practices of Shevet, please contact us at shevetteam@gmail.com.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted within the app, and the effective date will be changed accordingly.

By using Shevet, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined in this Privacy Policy. Please check this policy regularly for any updates or changes.

Thank you for choosing Shevet and trusting us with your information. Your privacy is important to us.
